If you haven't travelled, you haven't lived ...

Here is where I share stories about the places I have travelled to, experiences I have had on my travels, tips and tricks about destinations I have been, hotel reviews, living out of a suitcase and much more. If there is a particular destination you would like me to write about, please send me an email, I am always happy to discuss!
